Point of view

The followings are my point of view that I collect over time. You can say it the motto of my life.

The right tool

Software development is diverse. There are many ways, tools to do archive the same goal. One should have curiosity to learn different tools, have encouragement to change the toolset, have consideration to select the toolset.

The gap

Software development is complex. Over time, we have learnt to divide the work into different roles (product owner, designer, tester, devops...). One who has the mix role knowledge tends to do the better job. Developer who has tester experience always have higher output quality. One should have insight to learn different roles in exchange for better work quality.

Crafmantship

Software development is building. One should learn to build from scratch without any reference. With crafmantship, one surely goes far.

The 80-20 Rule

80% of outputs result from 20% of inputs

Software development is learning. But there are too many things to learn. Remember the 80-20 rule. Do learn smarter. Always be learning. Never be afraid of working on new things.
